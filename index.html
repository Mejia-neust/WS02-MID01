About Alladdin Magi

One of the four Magi in the current era and a Magician. He also has a calm temperament, even able to stay level-headed in dangerous or dire situations to think up of plans and solutions.

Alladdin's Characteristics

  • All previous abilities in addition to Law Manipulation and Physics Manipulation
