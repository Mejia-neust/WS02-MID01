Dan Heng

About Dan Heng

Dan Heng (Chinese: 丹恒) is a playable character in Honkai Star Rail. The cold and reserved train guard and archivist of the Astral Express.

Dan Heng's Characteristics

  • Stellar Warp (Indefinite)
  • Departure Warp (Indefinite until all 50 warps are made)
  • He can be obtained for free during Trailblaze Mission Eye of the Storm in the Chapter Today Is Yesterday's Tomorrow.
